About

After a successful career as in the Automobile Industry, he chose to retire early to dedicate himself fully to his true passion, the Bansuri (Indian classical bamboo flute). Trained in the rich tradition of Indian classical music under a direct disciple of the legendary Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia, he has honed his art over many years.

With extensive experience performing at cultural venues and music gatherings across Nashik, he now shares his knowledge and love for the flute through dedicated teaching. His classes focus not only on technique, but also on helping students connect with the depth and beauty of Indian classical music.
